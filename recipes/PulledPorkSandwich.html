Pulled Pork Sandwich

An easy recipe for a delicious sandwich!

With a small bit of prep work, you can have a delicious meal or snack for every day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp of salt/pepper
  • 1/2 cup dijon mustard
  • 2-8lbs of pork shoulder roast
  • 1/2 cup barbeque sauce
  • 4-16 hamburger buns

Steps

  1. To start, we preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
  2. Next we mix brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Spread mustard over pork roast and sprinkle with brown sugar mixture, using the entire amount.
  4. Next we line a shallow baking dish with aluminum foil. Place a rack in the baking dish and arrange pork roast on rack.
  5. Allow pork to bake in the preheated oven until very tender, 9 to 11 hours. Let pork cool, then shred into bite-size pieces.
  6. Stir barbeque sauce into pork to moisten; serve with hamburger buns and whatever other toppings are desired.
