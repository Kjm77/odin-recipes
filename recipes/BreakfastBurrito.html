Breakfast Burrito

The perfect breakfast burrito!

Filled to the brim with crispy bacon, fluffy scrambled eggs, savory potatoes and melted cheeses. The ideal start to any day, and ready in ten minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 4-6 strips of bacon
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 3-4 potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of desired cheese
  • 1/2 tbsps of salt/pepper
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Dice potatoes into small cubes and lightly fry on a small frying pan with olive oil, salt and pepper.
  2. Remove potatoes from frying pan, add bacon to same pan; fry bacon until desired crispiness then remove, roughly chop and set aside.
  3. Prepare egg mixture in a bowl with dash of salt and pepper. Add mixture to large pan over medium heat and stir.
  4. Lightly brown the flour tortilla on second pan while the eggs finish cooking.
  5. Place warm tortilla on plate, then add ingredients: scrambled eggs, potatoes, bacon and cheese.
  6. Pinch both sides of tortilla inward then roll slowly and as tighly as possible. If desired burrito can also be toasted lightly on pan.
