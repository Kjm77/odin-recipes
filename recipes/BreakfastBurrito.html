Breakfast Burrito

The perfect breakfast burrito!

Filled to the brim with crispy bacon, fluffy scrambled eggs, savory potatoes and melted cheeses. The ideal start to any day, and ready in ten minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 4-6 strips of bacon
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 3-4 potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of desired cheese
  • 1/2 tbsps of salt/pepper
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. To start, we dice the potatoes into small cubes and lightly fry them on a small pan with a little olive oil, salt and pepper.
  2. After removing potatoes from the pan, we set them aside and add the strips of bacon to the same pan; fry bacon until desired level of crispiness then remove, roughly chop and set aside.
  3. Next up, we prepare the egg mixture in a bowl with a little salt, pepper and butter. After mixing thoroughly, we add this mixture to a large pan over medium heat and mix slowly.
  4. At this point, we use the smaller pan to lightly toast the flour tortilla while the eggs finish cooking.
  5. Once the scrambled eggs are at their fluffiest, we begin assembling the burrito. Place the warm tortilla on a plate, then add the ingredients: scrambled eggs, potatoes, bacon and then our cheeses on top.
  6. To roll the burrito, we pinch both sides inward then starting from one end we roll slowly and as tighly as possible. If desired burrito can also be toasted lightly on a pan.
